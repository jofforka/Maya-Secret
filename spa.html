FREQUENTLY ASKED

Booking notes.

Can I select more than one service?

Yes. Choose as many services as you need across the different categories. Your total price and estimated duration update automatically.

Is the reservation confirmed immediately?

No. The WhatsApp message is an appointment request. Your slot is confirmed when Maya’s Secret replies.

Can specialist services require consultation?

Yes. PRP Facial, O-Shot, tag removal and certain targeted treatments may require an assessment before confirmation.

Can I change my selected services later?

Yes. Discuss any changes with Maya’s Secret on WhatsApp before your appointment is confirmed.
